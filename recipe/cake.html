CHOCOLATE CAKE

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4cups sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions:

  1. preheat oven to 350F
  2. mix dry ingredients
  3. add wet ingredients,except boiling water.
  4. blend well,then stir in boiling water.
  5. pour into greased pans,bake 30-35 mins.
  6. cool,frost,and enjoy!
